Help & Support

How to Add a Book?

Go to the Dashboard and click on "Add Book". Fill in the details like title, author, category, and submit the form.

How to Issue a Book?

Select the "Issue Book" option, choose the book and the user, and confirm the issue request.

How to View Available Books?

Click on "Available Books" on the dashboard. You can see a list of all books currently in stock.

How to See Borrowed Books?

Navigate to the "Reports" section to view issued books, due dates, and return history.

How to Get Technical Support?

If you encounter any issues, contact the admin or email us at support@librarysystem.com.
